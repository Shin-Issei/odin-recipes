Cheese Steak

Everyones favorite greesy ball of joy, the cheese steak. What could be better than shredded steak, onions and cheese on a bun? Basically, nothing. Let's get started.

Ingredients

  • 6oz. of steak (unshredded)
  • 1 White Onion
  • 1 Slice of Yellow American Cheese
  • 1 Italian Steak Roll
  • Green Peppers
  • Some butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in the pan and cut the onions and peppers.
  2. Sautee the onions in the butter until slightly brown
  3. Once the onions are done add the steak in the same pan.
  4. Season the steak before you begin cooking.
  5. Cook the steak to the desired doneness.
  6. Remove the steak from the pan and let rest before shredding with knife and fork.
  7. Add the steak to the roll and then the onions.
  8. After that the cheese and green peppers and dig in.
