French Toast

A break fast classic that is fast, cheap and simple. All it takes is eggs bread and about 10 minutes (Coffee and orange juice is optional).

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in seperate bowl.
  2. Heat a small fry pan with 1oz of butter until melted.
  3. Coat each slice of bread with the beaten eggs and place in fry pan.
  4. Cook until egg is fused with the bread.
  5. Place cooked slices of toast on to plate and spread ground cinnamon on top
  6. Add syrup.
  7. Enjoy!
