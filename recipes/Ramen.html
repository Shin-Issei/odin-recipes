Instant Ramen With Beef

Who says that just becuase you're flat broke you can't enjoy some top quality food? I know my wallet says that all the time. Instead of top quality food let's have some top ramen with a little more dignity.

Ingredients

  • Thin Slices of Beef (any cut)
  • Nongshim Instant Ramen
  • 2 1/3 cups of filtered water
  • Seasoned Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the beef slices with salt and pepper and then sear them to preferred doneness.
  2. While the beef is cooking boil the water and add the noodles
  3. Ensure that the noodles don't cook for more than 2minutes or else they will be too gooey
  4. Kill the heat on the noodles at the 1:15 mark and add the flavor packet that came with the noodles.
  5. Put the steak slices in a bowl and then add the ramen on top.
  6. Stir and enjoy.
